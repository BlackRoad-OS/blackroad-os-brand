# Typography

BlackRoad OS typography pairs precision with clarity. JetBrains Mono handles technical narratives while Inter delivers clean reading for product copy.

## Font Stack

- **Headers & code**: JetBrains Mono, monospace
- **Body & UI**: Inter, system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Segoe UI", sans-serif
- **Fallback**: system-ui; never substitute decorative fonts.

## Hierarchy

- Title pages & hero headers: JetBrains Mono, 48–64px, semi-bold.
- Section headers: JetBrains Mono, 28–32px, semi-bold; keep title case for product names.
- Body copy: Inter, 16–18px, regular; increase to 20px for long-form docs.
- Captions & labels: Inter, 12–14px, medium; ensure adequate spacing from controls.

## Rules

- Do not distort or stretch logotypes or wordmarks.
- Maintain generous line-height (1.4–1.6) on dense content.
- Align text on a 4px grid to match spacing tokens.
- Use monospace callouts for commands, code, and SIG diagrams to highlight the visual identity of the Road.
