# Logo Protection Zones

Preserve clarity and authority of every BlackRoad OS mark with strict safe areas and sizing.

## Safe Area

- Minimum clear space equals the height of the "OS" letters in the lockup.
- Apply the same inset on all sides around the logo, including top and bottom.
- No text, imagery, or UI controls may intrude into this zone.

## Minimum Sizes

- Digital: 48px height for the primary lockup; 32px for the monogram.
- Print: 18mm height for the primary lockup; 12mm for the monogram.

## Backgrounds

- Preferred: `--br-black` or `--br-surface` with subtle vignette (`--gradient-road-horizon`).
- Allowed: solid dark imagery with enough negative space.
- Avoid: busy patterns, noisy photos, or gradients that clash with the mark.

## Misuse

- Do not distort, recolor, or add effects beyond approved glows.
- Do not rotate or skew.
- Do not place over low-contrast backgrounds or neon noise.

## Asset Sources

- Use official files from `/brand-kit/logos` and `/assets/svg`.
- Keep vector formats (SVG, PDF) as the default; use PNG only for raster previews.
