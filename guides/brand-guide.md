# Brand Guide

BlackRoad OS is the visual identity kernel of the Road. The look is minimal, neon, and sharply technical—built to feel like precision light cutting through darkness.

## Core Identity

- **Philosophy**: "The Neon Road" — a path of signal light through black space.
- **Voice**: Confident, technical, exacting; avoid hype and clutter.
- **Mood**: Dark, high-contrast, and intentional with selective glow.

## Palette & Gradients

- Base on `--br-black` and `--br-surface`; keep neon accents controlled.
- Primary gradients: `--gradient-br-os` for flows, `--gradient-road-horizon` for depth, `--gradient-sig-spiral` for SIG-inspired arcs.
- Avoid random gradient angles; keep direction aligned with layout grids.

## Typography

- JetBrains Mono for headers, labels, and code to highlight the system-first mindset.
- Inter for body copy to keep long-form reading effortless.
- Maintain generous spacing and clear hierarchy as defined in `/brand/typography.md`.

## Imagery

- **Themes**: neon horizon, fractal roads, quantum geometry, black rooms with glowing glyphs, gold/teal/pink spectral lines.
- **Avoid**: cartoonish shapes, clutter, inconsistent shadows, or unanchored gradients.
- Pair imagery with generous negative space to preserve the visual identity of the Road.

## Layout & Spacing

- Build on the 4px spacing rhythm from `/brand/spacing.md`.
- Maintain safe zones around logos and hero statements; minimum padding equals logo height.
- Favor edge-aligned grids and consistent optical margins.

## Usage Rules Summary

- Use official assets from `/brand-kit` and `/assets` only; never recreate logos.
- Keep applications dark-first; light backgrounds are allowed only with strong contrast and controlled accents.
- When combining with partner brands, BlackRoad elements remain primary and undistorted.
