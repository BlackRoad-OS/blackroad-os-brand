Usage Guidelines

Do

  • Use the official color palette
  • Maintain clear space around the logo
  • Use dark backgrounds for primary contexts
  • Apply gradients for emphasis
  • Keep text highly readable

Don't

  • Alter the logo colors or proportions
  • Use low contrast text combinations
  • Place logo on busy backgrounds
  • Stretch or distort brand elements
  • Use unofficial color variations
