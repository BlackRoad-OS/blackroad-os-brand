# Email Templates

This file contains guidelines and templates for BlackRoad OS email communications.

## What Belongs Here

- **Email newsletter templates**: HTML templates for regular newsletters
- **Marketing email templates**: Promotional and announcement emails
- **Transactional email templates**: Account-related and system emails
- **Event invitation templates**: Templates for event announcements and invitations
- **Email signatures**: Standardized signature formats for team members

## Template Types

### Newsletter Template
- Header with logo and branding
- Featured content section
- News and updates sections
- Footer with social links and unsubscribe

### Marketing/Announcement Template
- Eye-catching header
- Clear call-to-action
- Product/feature highlights
- Branded footer

### Transactional Email Template
- Clean, minimal design
- Clear messaging
- Account-specific information
- Support contact information

### Email Signature Template
- Name and title
- Contact information
- BlackRoad OS logo
- Social media links (optional)

## Design Guidelines

- **Responsive design**: Ensure templates work on mobile and desktop
- **Brand consistency**: Use official colors, fonts, and logo from brand kit
- **Accessibility**: Maintain proper contrast ratios and readable font sizes
- **Plain text alternative**: Always provide plain text versions
- **Testing**: Test across major email clients (Gmail, Outlook, Apple Mail, etc.)

## Technical Specifications

- Maximum width: 600-650px for best compatibility
- Use inline CSS for styling
- Optimize images for email delivery
- Include alt text for all images
- Test with spam filters

## Best Practices

- Keep subject lines concise and compelling
- Personalize when possible
- Include clear unsubscribe options
- Follow CAN-SPAM and GDPR requirements
- Refer to `/guides/usage-rules.md` for brand usage guidelines
