Usage notes

Density · Hierarchy

Spacing

Use a consistent rhythm: 20px card padding, 12–16px gaps between elements, and at least 24px between stacked cards. Cards should feel airy even on dense dashboards.

Hierarchy

Neon appears on primary actions, key stats, and modal confirms. Most containers stay monochrome so the gradients mean something when they show up.

When to use what

  • • Use cards for content blocks and summaries.
  • • Use panels for page-level layout (shells, sidebars, footers).
  • • Use modals only when the decision blocks everything else.
