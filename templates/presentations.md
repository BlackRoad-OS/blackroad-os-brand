# Presentation Templates

This file contains guidelines and templates for creating presentations about BlackRoad OS.

## What Belongs Here

- **PowerPoint/Keynote templates**: Slide deck templates with branded layouts
- **Google Slides templates**: Cloud-based presentation templates
- **Pitch deck templates**: Templates for investor or partner presentations
- **Event presentation templates**: Templates for conferences, meetups, and events
- **Internal presentation templates**: Templates for team meetings and internal communications

## Template Types

### General Presentation Template
- Standard slide layouts (title, content, two-column, etc.)
- Branded color schemes and fonts
- Logo placement guidelines
- Image and diagram layouts

### Product Demo Template
- Product showcase layouts
- Screenshot guidelines
- Feature highlight slides
- Call-to-action slides

### Event/Conference Template
- Speaker introduction slides
- Agenda and schedule layouts
- Q&A and discussion slides

## Guidelines

- Always use official brand colors from the brand kit
- Include the BlackRoad OS logo on appropriate slides
- Follow typography guidelines for headings and body text
- Maintain consistent spacing and alignment
- Refer to `/guides/usage-rules.md` for detailed brand usage rules

## File Formats

Templates should be provided in multiple formats:
- `.pptx` (PowerPoint)
- `.key` (Keynote)
- Link to Google Slides template
- PDF version for reference
